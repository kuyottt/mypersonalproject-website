About Me

Hi! I am Mary Grace Rivera, a digital animator and illustrator located in Camarines Sur. I am currently studying in Naga City, where I am pursuing a Bachelor of Science degree in Entertainment and Multimedia Computing at Bicol State College of Applied Sciences and Technology. I specialize in various art fields such as writing, graphic design, and others. Additionally, I am studying 3D modeling and digital illustration. My ultimate goal is to become an animator, and I am determined to do everything in my power to improve my skills, expand my knowledge, and connect with fellow aspiring animators. I have one guiding principle: I create only when there's something meaningful to convey. My art is my statement, and it will always remain so.
